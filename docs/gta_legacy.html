Evolution of the GTA Universe

Grand Theft Auto

1997
🚕

The retro 2D top-down legacy that started it all, introducing high-speed carjacking runs and sandbox freedom across Liberty City, San Andreas, and Vice City.

Grand Theft Auto 2

1999
⚡

Set in a futuristic metropolis, this top-down classic introduced the respect meter, forcing players to manage reputation with rival gang syndicates.

Grand Theft Auto III

2001
🌆

The revolutionary shift to full 3D. Liberty City became a living, breathing sandbox, setting the standard for all modern open-world video games.

GTA: Vice City

2002
🌴

Tommy Vercetti's rise to power in a retro 80s neon paradise. Styled with legendary synthwave radio stations and Miami hotel backdrops.

GTA: San Andreas

2004
🚲

CJ, Grove Street, and a massive state containing Los Santos, San Fierro, and Las Venturas. Set a benchmark in character customization and map scale.

Grand Theft Auto IV

2008
🌉

Niko Bellic and the dark, gritty realism of modern Liberty City. Features cutting-edge physics and mature narrative storytelling.

Grand Theft Auto V

2013
💵

The record-breaking masterpiece featuring three playable characters (Michael, Franklin, Trevor) in modern Los Santos, and the massive GTA Online universe.

Grand Theft Auto VI

TBA
🌅

Lucia, Jason, and the next-generation return to Leonida/Vice City. Set to push the boundaries of immersive gaming tech to their limits.
